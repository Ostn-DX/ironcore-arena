## STRATEGIC MISSION BREAKDOWN (16-29)

**Not just "kill everything" — these require tactics:**

### Mission 16: Artillery Strike
- 4 Artillery units at map corners
- They fire at you constantly
- Must reach and destroy them before they destroy you
- **Strategy:** Fast scouts to rush artilleries

### Mission 17: Siege Warfare
- 3 Command centers to destroy
- Heavy defenses around each
- Can attack in any order
- **Strategy:** Tank push vs. flank with speed

### Mission 18: Nightmare
- 10 minute survival
- All 12 enemy types attack
- Waves get harder
- **Strategy:** Balanced team, repair units

### Mission 21: Beachhead
- Capture 3 points simultaneously
- Enemies counter-attack
- **Strategy:** Split forces or sequential?

### Mission 22: Convoy
- 3 civilian bots must survive
- Escort across large map
- Enemies ambush
- **Strategy:** Defensive tanks + aggressive interceptors

### Mission 23: Scorched Earth
- 5 structures to destroy
- Spread across map
- **Strategy:** Strike teams or full push?

### Mission 24: King of the Hill
- Hold center zone
- Constant enemy waves
- **Strategy:** Fortify position or mobile defense?

## BUILD VERSATILITY REQUIREMENTS

**No single meta build can beat all missions:**

| Mission Type | Optimal Build | Why |
|--------------|---------------|-----|
| Speed runs | Fast scouts | Reach targets quickly |
| Survival | Heavy tanks + repair | Outlast damage |
| Boss fights | High DPS | Race against mechanics |
| Artillery | Fast flanking | Avoid fire, reach backline |
| Defense | Tanks + range | Hold position |
| Escort | Mixed | Flexibility needed |

**Forced Adaptation:**
- Some missions have weight caps that favor light builds
- Others need heavy armor to survive
- Some need speed to complete objectives
- Boss missions need pure damage
